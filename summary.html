Our Itinerary (so far)

By Mike

Friday, May 25: We left from Syracuse to my apartment in Rochester.

Sunday, May 27: Drove from Rochester to the Southern most end of the Allegheny State Forest in Pennsylvania. Stayed at a Free campsite.

Tuesday, May 29: Drove from Allegheny to Columbus, Ohio to stay with Tom's Uncle Tom.

Wednesday, May 30: Left Columbus and drove through Kentucky just into Indiana, where we stayed at a free campsite in Hoosier National Forest right along the Ohio River.

Thursday, May 31: Left Hoosier and drove into Kentucky to Mammoth Caves National Park, where we stayed at the paid park campgrounds.

Saturday, June 2: Drove from Mammoth Caves South into Nashville Tennessee, where we stayed near five points with Mike's Pledge Brother Konar Chapman.

Monday, June 4: From Nashville along route 40 (which Tom loves) to Smokey Mountain National Forest, where we stayed at the paid Cosby Campground, and hiked along the Appalachian Trail to Mount Camerer.

Tuesday, June 5: Drove through the Smokey Mountains Park into North Carolina, then South into the Nantahala Forest, where we stayed at the free Wine Springs Horse Camp.

Wednesday, June 6: Arrived in Norcross Georgia, a Suburb of Atlanta. We stayed with Mike's family-friend Mike Morell.

Thursday, June 7: Tom flies back to Syracuse for a Family Wedding.

Monday, June 11: Tom returns to Atlanta. Mike is sick so we stay two extra days.

Wednesday June 13: Leave Atlanta And Drive South to Grassy Point Park on Escribano Point in Florida, just East of Pensacola.
